# Disclosures (summarized)
- **Pre-existing conditions** are not covered.
- **Waiting periods** apply: e.g., 14 days for A&I; preventive starts the day after enrollment. State variations apply.
- **Annual deductible, co-insurance, and benefit limits** affect reimbursement amount.
- **Money-back guarantee** (if no claims) varies by state; not available everywhere (e.g., NY). Confirm gating.
- Product/discount availability varies by state/underwriter. See sample policy for full terms.
