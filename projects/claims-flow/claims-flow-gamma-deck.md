# Reimagining Claims at Spot
## A Strategic Initiative to Drive Retention Through Claims Excellence

---

## Why This Matters

**Claims satisfaction is our #1 retention driver**

- 45% of cancellations cite denied claims
- 28% disappointed with reimbursement amounts
- Current: 85-87% approval rate, 6-7 day processing, ~50% direct deposit
- Strategic Priority #3: App & Claims Experience Redesign

---

## The Vision

**Current State:**
Claims buried in portal, form-heavy, limited visibility, reactive support

**Future State:**
Photo-first AI submission, always-visible progress bar, proactive pre-vet education

**Core Principle:**
Transparency, proactive support, contextual help, respect customer choices

---

## Submitting a New Claim
### Entry Point & Photo-First Upload

**Big "Submit Claim" button on dashboard** - prominent, unmissable

**Photo-first submission:**
- Invoice photos + Medical records (conditional)
- AI validates pet name, hospital, vet notes
- Multiple photos, guidance modal with examples

## Tracking Open Claims
### Always-Visible Progress Bar

**Active claim = Progress bar at TOP of dashboard**
- Shows current status at a glance
- Multiple claims: Auto-cycle every 5 seconds with dots indicator

**Four stages:**
1. Received ‚úì
2. Under Review (IPG reviewing)
3. Processing (Calculating reimbursement)
4. Payment Sent

## Progress Bar States & "Get More Info"

**States:**
- Standard (blue), Needs Action (yellow/orange), Completed (green), Delayed (grey)

**"Get More Info" button opens modal with:**
- Detailed status explanation
- What's happening behind the scenes
- All submitted claim details
- Timeline with estimated dates
- Click-to-call: 1-700-449-4848

## Push Notifications

**Multi-stage notifications with deep links:**

- **Received:** "We received your claim for [Pet Name]! We'll review it within 24 hours."
- **Under review:** "Good news! We're reviewing [Pet Name]'s claim now."
- **Action needed:** "We need more info for [Pet Name]'s claim. Tap to add it now."
- **Approved:** "Great news! [Pet Name]'s claim is approved. $XXX will arrive in [X] days."
- **Denied:** "[Pet Name]'s claim was not approved. Tap to learn why and appeal options."

## When Additional Info Is Needed

**Immediate multi-channel notification** (push + email + SMS)

**Action needed UI state:**
- Progress bar turns yellow/orange with "Action Needed" badge
- Red dot on app icon

**Clear instructions with:**
- Specific request ("upload medical records for [condition]")
- Rationale ("verify not pre-existing condition")
- Deadline to avoid delays

**In-app addition:** Same photo upload flow, AI validation, text note option

---

## Key Takeaways

**Five Major Insights:**
1. Claims satisfaction IS retention
2. Proactive beats reactive (educate before vet visit)
3. Transparency builds trust (visible status > email)
4. Photo-first eliminates form friction
5. Respect choices, don't nag
