## SLIDE 8: Common Questions Answered

**Q: "What if my pet already has a health issue?"**
A: Pre-existing conditions aren't covered, BUT any NEW conditions after your policy starts ARE covered. That's why enrolling NOW protects your pet's future.

**Q: "Can I cancel anytime?"**
A: Yes! No long-term contracts. Cancel anytime (though 93.6% of customers stay past 30 days).

**Q: "What about dental coverage?"**
A: Accident-related dental is covered. Add Wellness for routine dental cleanings!

**Q: "Do rates increase?"**
A: Premiums may adjust as pets age (like human insurance), but you keep your employee discount for life.

**Q: "Which vets accept Spot?"**
A: ALL licensed veterinarians! No networks, no restrictions.
