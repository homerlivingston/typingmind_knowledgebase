# Journey Performance Metrics - Spot Pet Insurance

## Strategic Metrics Framework for $350M GWP

### Performance Philosophy
**Mission Integration:** Journey metrics directly measure progress toward helping pets and their parents lead longer, healthier, and happier lives while optimizing the Conversion × Retention × AOV formula for $350M GWP achievement.

**Data-Driven Decision Making:** All journey metrics support immediate GWP impact through systematic optimization of UTQ, QTP, and retention rates across customer touchpoints.

## Stage 5: Claims Experience Metrics (Primary Retention Driver)

### Claims Process Performance (External Underwriter Constraints)
**Current Claims KPIs:**
- **Processing Time:** 6-7 days average (5 controllable days at Spot)
- **Success Rate:** 87-99% of claims processed successfully (monthly variation)
- **Average Claim Value:** $430.97 (much lower than industry average)
- **Automation Rate:** Limited to "open and shut" claims only

### Claims Satisfaction Impact on Retention
**Churn Analysis (Cancellation Survey Data):**
- **Claim Denials:** 45% of cancellations cite denied claims (pre-existing condition disputes)
- **Rate Increases:** 25% cite premium increases over time
- **Low Reimbursements:** 28% disappointed with payout amounts vs expectations
- **Process Frustration:** Documentation requests, communication delays

**Claims Experience Correlation:**
- **Strongest Retention Predictor:** Claims satisfaction directly correlates with renewal
- **Operational Challenge:** Limited control over claims (IPG underwriter manages process)
- **Focus Opportunity:** Customer-facing experience improvements within available influence

### Claims Processing Optimization Targets
**Performance Improvement Goals:**
- **Processing Time:** Reduce from 6-7 days to <5 days total
- **First-Time Approval:** Maintain >95% consistently vs current 87-99% variation
- **Communication Quality:** Proactive status updates vs reactive approach
- **Self-Service Adoption:** >70% mobile app claims submissions
