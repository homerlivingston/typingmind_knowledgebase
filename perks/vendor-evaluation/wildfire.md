# Wildfire Systems - Spot Perks 3.0 Vendor Analysis

**Demo Date:** October 2025
**Status:** Evaluation Phase
**Primary Contact:** Aimee (Wildfire)
**Internal Champion:** Steven

---

## Executive Summary

Wildfire Systems is a white-label monetization platform offering cashback rewards, browser extensions, and marketplace solutions. They provide turnkey access to 50,000+ merchant relationships, eliminating the need for Spot to individually manage partner deals. This evaluation is part of Spot's strategy to turn perks into a true differentiator over the next couple of years.

---

## Company Overview

### About Wildfire
- **Platform Type:** Patented white-label rewards and cashback platform
- **Core Offering:** Social commerce, rewards, and cashback integrated into existing customer journeys
- **Merchant Network:** 50,000+ top-tier online stores worldwide
- **Security:** Bank-grade security with SOC-2 and PCI-DSS compliance
- **Business Model:** Revenue share with enterprise partners

### Notable Clients
- **Financial Services:** Citi, RBC, Visa
- **Fintech:** Acorns
- **Enterprise:** Microsoft
- Multiple large banking organizations worldwide

### Technology Innovation
- First platform to enable cashback on AI-assisted shopping (Gemini, Copilot, ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity)
- First white-label mobile Safari extension for iOS 15
- Supports Chrome, Edge, and Safari across desktop and mobile

## Solution Components for Spot Perks 3.0

### 1. Marketplace (Turnkey Solution)
**Description:** Co-branded marketplace similar to LifeMart

**Features:**
- Fully managed merchant relationships
- Turnkey implementation
- White-label design with Spot branding
- Access to complete merchant network

**Benefits:**
- Eliminates need to negotiate individual partnerships
- Significantly expands available perks
- Wildfire manages deal sourcing and maintenance
- Faster time to market

### 2. Browser Extension (Shopping Companion)
**Description:** Honey-style browser extension branded as "Spot Perks"

**Supported Platforms:**
- Chrome (desktop)
- Edge (desktop)
- Safari (desktop)
- Mobile Safari (iOS)

**Core Functionality:**
- Automated coupon discovery and application
- Real-time cashback rewards
- Contextual deal alerts during shopping
- Works across 50,000+ merchant sites

**Strategic Benefits:**
- Top-of-mind brand awareness during shopping
- Customer behavior visibility and data
- Reminds customers they have Spot insurance
- Additional touchpoint in customer journey

**Key Test Question:** Does the browser extension help or hurt us as a brand reminder?

### 3. API Integration (Custom Implementation)
**Description:** Consume Wildfire API for custom perks experience

**Features:**
- Flexible integration into existing Spot systems
- Custom UX/UI control
- Direct access to merchant deals and cashback offers
- Programmatic deal management

**Use Case:** Allows Spot to build custom perks experiences while leveraging Wildfire's merchant network

### 4. Additional Capabilities
- Cash back programs
- Rewards point systems
- Digital coupons
- Custom loyalty programs

## Strategic Considerations

### Path to Exclusivity
**Critical Question:** Can Spot become the only pet insurance provider in Wildfire's network?

**Benefits of Exclusivity:**
- Competitive differentiation in pet insurance market
- Prevents competitors (Healthy Paws, Trupanion, Lemonade, etc.) from accessing same perks
- Strengthens Spot's unique value proposition
- Justifies higher marketing spend on perks program

**Negotiation Points:**
- Industry exclusivity terms
- Contract duration requirements
- Performance minimums
- Co-marketing opportunities

### Value Proposition for Spot

**Customer Acquisition:**
- Perks as differentiator in purchase decision
- Enhanced value perception beyond insurance coverage
- Competitive advantage if exclusive

**Customer Retention:**
- Increased engagement through browser extension
- Regular brand touchpoints during shopping
- Additional value reinforcement
- Behavioral data for personalization

**Revenue Potential:**
- Possible revenue share from cashback transactions
- Affiliate commission opportunities
- Data insights for marketing optimization

**Operational Efficiency:**
- Eliminates partner sourcing overhead
- No individual deal negotiation required
- Wildfire manages merchant relationships
- Scalable perks program without headcount increase

## Latest Call Updates (October 23, 2025)

### Implementation Flexibility Confirmed

**Pilot Program Structure:**
- Wildfire supports **3-6 month pilot** on "shopping wall" before full rollout
- Low-risk testing approach before major commitment
- Allows validation of engagement and retention impact

**Reward Model Options:**
- **Not required to use cashback** - can frame as "Spot Points" or any internal reward currency
- Supports pure discount code/coupon center model
- Mix of discounts (30%) and cashback (70%) in current network
- Backend conversion flexibility (dollars → Spot Points)

**Custom Offers Integration:**
- Can add Spot's existing partner offers into Wildfire marketplace
- Requires partners to onboard with Wildfire's system (some friction)
- If merchant not in network, Wildfire can facilitate affiliate setup
- Spot can curate which offers appear in our marketplace

### Technical Implementation Options

**Option A - Turnkey Solution (6-8 weeks):**
- Use Wildfire's existing UI/codebase
- Link out to their interface
- Fastest deployment path
- Fully documented and proven

**Option B - Custom Build (3-6 months):**
- Connect to Wildfire APIs directly
- Build custom Spot-branded marketplace experience
- More control but longer timeline
- Wildfire believes turnkey is faster path to value

**Recommended Approach:** Start with turnkey, migrate to custom later if needed

### Revenue Generation Opportunities

**1. Partner Revenue Share:**
- Negotiate commission split on brands Spot brings to network
- Example: If Spot adds Fi collars, earn % on all Fi sales (even through other portals)
- Creates new revenue stream from existing partnerships

**2. Affiliate Network Participation:**
- Spot could become affiliate in Wildfire's broader network
- Drive pet insurance conversions through other shopping portals
- Marketing team opportunity

**3. Marketplace Real Estate:**
- Brands can bid for premium placement in Spot's shopping portal
- High-affinity pet brands pay for featured positioning
- Additional monetization layer

**4. Employee Benefits Platform:**
- Wildfire powers employee reward programs for payroll/benefits companies (ADP, etc.)
- Potential distribution channel for Spot insurance products

### Compliance Considerations

**Action Required:**
- Confirm cashback legality and regulatory requirements
- Validate "Spot Points" framing from compliance perspective
- Review reward structure with legal team
