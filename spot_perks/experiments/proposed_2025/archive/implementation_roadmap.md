# Spot Perks Implementation Roadmap & Prioritization

**Strategic Objective**: Systematic deployment to recover retention performance from 4.5% to 11% churn reduction, maximizing early wins while building toward transformational impact.

---

## PRIORITIZATION METHODOLOGY

### **Evaluation Criteria**
1. **Retention Impact Potential** (40% weight): Direct effect on 3-6 month churn rate
2. **Implementation Speed** (25% weight): Time to deployment and results
3. **Resource Requirements** (20% weight): Development, marketing, analytics effort
4. **Learning Value** (10% weight): Insights for future experiments
5. **Risk Level** (5% weight): Potential for negative impact or rollback complexity

### **Prioritization Scoring**
- **Priority 1 (Immediate)**: Launch within 2-4 weeks, foundational for program
- **Priority 2 (Near-term)**: Launch within 4-8 weeks, build on early wins
- **Priority 3 (Strategic)**: Launch within 8-16 weeks, transformational impact
- **Priority 4 (Exploratory)**: Launch based on earlier results, validate hypotheses

## RISK MITIGATION STRATEGIES

### **Technical Risk Management**
- **Progressive Rollout**: Test complex experiments with 10-20% of customers first
- **Rollback Capability**: Ensure all changes can be reversed within 24 hours
- **Performance Monitoring**: Real-time alerting for system impact or customer experience degradation
- **Parallel Development**: Maintain existing systems while building new capabilities

### **Business Risk Mitigation**
- **Control Groups**: Maintain measurement capability throughout all experiments
- **Stakeholder Communication**: Weekly updates on progress and early results
- **Customer Impact**: Monitor customer satisfaction and support ticket volume
- **Competitive Intelligence**: Track competitor perk program changes and market response

### **Resource Risk Planning**
- **Development Capacity**: Buffer time for unexpected complexity or bugs
- **Marketing Bandwidth**: Coordinate with other marketing initiatives and campaigns
- **Analytics Support**: Ensure measurement capability keeps pace with experiment deployment
- **External Dependencies**: Plan for potential Resonate, Customer.io, or partner integration delays
