# Spot Pet Insurance Perks Program 3.0 - Enhanced Product Brief

## Executive Summary

Spot Pet Insurance is launching an advanced perks platform powered by Wildfire's shopping rewards infrastructure. This multi-platform ecosystem transforms pet owners' everyday shopping into valuable rewards while generating revenue for Spot and actionable business intelligence for strategic partnerships. ## Testing & Validation Framework

### Phase 1 Browser Extension Testing Protocol

**A/B Test Design & Methodology**
- **Sample Size**: Minimum 10,000 customers per group (20,000 total)
- **Randomization**: Stratified random sampling by customer segment (new vs existing, policy type, geographic region)
- **Test Duration**: 90 days minimum with 30-day follow-up period
- **Success Criteria**: Statistical significance (p < 0.05) for primary metrics

**Primary Success Metrics**
- **Adoption Rate**: ≥25% of test group installs extension within 30 days
- **Retention Impact**: ≥50 basis points improvement in renewal rates for active users
- **Engagement**: ≥40% of installers use extension monthly
- **Revenue Impact**: Positive ROI within 6 months

**Secondary Success Metrics**
- Customer satisfaction scores (NPS improvement)
- Support ticket volume (should not increase significantly)
- Cross-platform usage distribution
- Deal activation and redemption rates

**Risk Mitigation & Contingency Planning**
- **Early Warning System**: Automated alerts if metrics fall below thresholds
- **Rollback Plan**: Ability to disable extension for test group if critical issues arise
- **Control Group Protection**: Ensure control group receives equivalent value through other channels
- **Data Privacy**: Ensure all testing complies with privacy regulations

**Testing Timeline & Milestones**
- **Week 1-2**: Technical deployment and initial monitoring setup
- **Week 3-4**: Soft launch to 10% of test group, monitor for critical issues
- **Week 5-8**: Full test group rollout, daily monitoring and optimization
- **Week 9-12**: Data collection and preliminary analysis
- **Week 13-16**: Full analysis, decision on broader rollout
