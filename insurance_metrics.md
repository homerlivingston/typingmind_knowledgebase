# Insurance Metrics - Spot Pet Insurance

## Strategic Performance Framework

### Gross Written Premium (GWP) Growth Trajectory
**Current Performance:** $8M monthly GWP addition (~$96M annual run rate)
**Target:** $350M GWP by February 2026
**Growth Strategy:** Combination of increased volume and higher AOV, with flexibility to prioritize volume over retention quality as deadline approaches
**Seasonal Factors:** Expected GWP acceleration during Nov-Dec open enrollment period for employee benefits

## Core Conversion Metrics - The GWP Growth Formula

### Primary Conversion Funnel Performance
**Key Metrics Framework:** Conversion × Retention × AOV = GWP Growth

#### User-to-Quote (UTQ) Performance
**Current Overall Rate:** 13%
**Definition:** Users landing on any landing page or main site who complete step 1 of quote form
**Optimization Focus:** A/B testing CTAs, images, copy, and user journey logic to maximize quote form entries
**Channel Variation:** Performance varies significantly by acquisition channel (see channel_level_comparison.csv data)

#### Quote-to-Policy (QTP) Performance
**Current Overall Rate:** 22%
**Definition:** Users completing initial quote form step who follow through to purchase
**Recent Success:** Spot Perks display optimization achieved 14% QTP increase
**Strategic Priority:** Continuous testing of quote form optimization and purchase funnel improvements

## Average Order Value (AOV) & Customer Economics

### Current AOV Performance
**Overall Monthly AOV:** $47
**Strategic Difference by Species:**
- **Dogs:** $52.78 monthly premium (prioritized for acquisition)
- **Cats:** $31.41 monthly premium (lower priority)
**Business Impact:** Dog policy acquisition prioritized in testing and feature development due to 68% higher AOV

### AOV Optimization Strategy
**Primary Levers:**
- Plan configuration upselling during quote process
- Annual billing adoption (improves both AOV and retention)
- Wellness add-on adoption
**Testing Approach:** Data-driven optimization of upsell messaging and placement throughout user journey
