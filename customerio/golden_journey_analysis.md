## Canada vs US Performance Gap

### The Canada Advantage

| Metric | Canada | US | Canada Advantage |
|--------|--------|-----|------------------|
| **Open Rate** | 37.87% | 23.72% | **+60%** |
| **Click Rate** | 10.75% | 5.51% | **+95%** |
| **Click-to-Open** | 28.39% | 23.24% | **+22%** |
| **Delivery** | 99.83% | 98.47% | **+1.4%** |
| **Unsubscribe** | 0.17% | 0.26% | **-35%** |

### Why Does Canada Perform Better?

**Possible Factors:**

**1. Market Maturity**
- Canada pet insurance market less saturated
- Fewer competitors = less email fatigue
- Earlier stage = more engaged customers

**2. Content Relevance**
- Canada-specific content (regulations, vets, claims process)
- Localized messaging resonates better
- Cultural alignment with communication style

**3. Customer Quality**
- Newer market = early adopters
- More motivated, engaged pet parents
- Higher average commitment

**4. List Hygiene**
- Smaller list = better maintained
- Newer customers = less churn/fatigue
- Higher data quality

**5. Sample Size Effect**
- 4,816 emails vs 478,950 emails
- Smaller sample may show higher variance
- Not statistically comparable due to volume difference

### Learning Opportunity

**If Canada performance is replicable:**
- US could achieve 37.87% open rate (currently 23.72%)
- US could achieve 10.75% click rate (currently 5.51%)
- **Potential uplift:** +60% opens, +95% clicks

**Actions:**
1. **Content audit:** What makes Canada emails more engaging?
2. **Messaging comparison:** How do themes differ?
3. **Journey structure:** Is Canada journey shorter/longer?
4. **Test Canadian approach** in US market (A/B test)
