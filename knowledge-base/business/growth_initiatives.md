## Primary Product Development Pipeline

### Highest Impact Development Initiatives

#### Add-a-Pet Journey Optimization (Priority #1)
**Strategic Objective:** Increase household penetration and customer lifetime value
**GWP Impact:** Drives AOV growth through multi-pet customer acquisition
**Data-Driven Hypothesis:** IF we optimize the add-a-pet user journey THEN we increase household penetration BECAUSE existing customers have highest conversion propensity AS MEASURED BY add-a-pet conversion rates and multi-pet household AOV
**Expected Performance:** Improved customer lifetime value and retention through expanded household coverage

#### Member Portal Revamp (Priority #2)
**Strategic Objective:** Enhance customer engagement and retention through improved digital experience
**GWP Impact:** Retention improvement through better customer portal experience
**Retention Connection:** Improved portal engagement correlates with successful claims filing (strongest retention predictor)
**Performance Target:** Increased member center registration and engagement as retention proxy behaviors

#### App & Claims Experience Redesign (Priority #3)
**Strategic Objective:** Streamline claims experience to improve retention
**Operational Constraint:** Limited control over claims department (managed by external underwriter)
**Strategic Approach:** Focus on customer-facing claims experience improvements within available influence
**Automated Claims Integration:** Leverage existing automation for "open and shut" claims processing

#### Embeddable Quote Form Development (Priority #4)
**Strategic Objective:** Improve partner integration experience and conversion rates
**Partner Impact:** Address API integration issues affecting aggregator performance (53% of monthly policies)
**Technical Benefit:** Simplified partner implementation reducing technical friction
**Performance Expected:** Improved UTQ and QTP rates across partner channels
