# Customer Experience Journey - Spot Pet Insurance

## Strategic Journey Framework for $350M GWP

### Journey Philosophy
**Mission Integration:** Customer journey optimization directly supports helping pets and their parents lead longer, healthier, and happier lives while driving the Conversion × Retention × AOV formula for $350M GWP target achievement.

**Data-Driven Approach:** Every journey touchpoint measured and optimized using real performance data to maximize immediate GWP impact through improved UTQ, QTP, and retention rates.

## Journey Performance Overview (Current State)

### Core Journey Metrics (August 2025 Data)
**Conversion Funnel Performance:**
- **New User → Quote:** 14.6% (UTQ rate)
- **Quote → Policy:** 22.0% (QTP rate)
- **End-to-End Conversion:** ~2.2% (compound conversion rate)
- **30-Day Retention:** 93.6% (6.4% early cancellation rate)
- **Channel Variation:** Organic 49.5% QTP vs Paid Social 15.3% QTP

**Channel Performance by Volume:**
- **Aggregators:** 56.8% of monthly policies, $280 CPA, 18.4% QTP
- **Direct Traffic:** Highest LTV $2,734, 7.6% QTP, premium customers
- **Organic Search:** $1 CPA, 49.5% QTP, highest efficiency
- **Employer Benefits:** $4 CPA, group discount leverage

## Stage 2: Research & Education

### Information Gathering Pain Points (Quoter Survey Data)
**Top Research Barriers (Preventing Conversion):**
- **Coverage Confusion:** 28% unclear "what is covered vs not covered"
- **Reimbursement Mystery:** 22% don't understand "how I would be reimbursed"
- **Terminology Barriers:** 15% unfamiliar with "reimbursement rate, deductible, annual limit"
- **Price Sensitivity:** #1 objection "policy was too expensive" despite competitive $51.47 average

### Research Journey Content Strategy
**Education Priority Content (Addresses Real Pain Points):**
- **Coverage Clarity:** Interactive coverage examples, "what's covered/not covered" clear explanations
- **Reimbursement Process:** Step-by-step claims reimbursement education with real examples
- **Terminology Guide:** Simple definitions of deductibles, reimbursement rates, annual limits
- **Value Communication:** Price positioning vs benefits to address "too expensive" objection

### Channel-Specific Research Behavior
**Organic Search (49.5% QTP):** Educational content consumption, comparison seeking, research-driven
**Aggregator Sites (18.4% QTP):** Price-focused comparison, competitive positioning critical
**Direct Traffic (7.6% QTP):** Brand-driven research, comprehensive evaluation, highest intent

## Stage 5: Claims Experience Journey (Primary Retention Driver)

### Claims Process Performance (External Underwriter Constraints)
**Current Claims Metrics:**
- **Processing Time:** 6-7 days average (5 controllable days at Spot)
- **Success Rate:** 87-99% of claims processed successfully (monthly variation)
- **Average Claim:** $430.97 (much lower than industry average)
- **Automation:** Available for "open and shut" claims only

### Claims Experience Pain Points (Cancellation Survey Data)
**Primary Churn Drivers:**
- **Claim Denials:** 45% of cancellations cite denied claims (pre-existing condition disputes)
- **Rate Increases:** 25% cite premium increases over time
- **Low Reimbursements:** 28% disappointed with payout amounts vs expectations
- **Process Frustration:** Documentation requests, slow processing, communication issues

### Claims Experience Optimization (Within Operational Constraints)
**Customer-Facing Improvements:**
- **Communication Enhancement:** Proactive status updates, clear timelines, expectation management
- **Documentation Support:** Better guidance on required claim documentation
- **Automated Processing:** Maximize utilization of existing automation for eligible claims
- **Mobile App Claims:** Streamlined submission process with OCR document processing

### Claims Satisfaction Impact on Retention
**Strategic Priority:** Claims experience is the strongest predictor of retention/churn
**Operational Challenge:** Limited control over claims department (managed by IPG underwriter)
**Focus Area:** Enhance customer-facing claims experience within available influence
