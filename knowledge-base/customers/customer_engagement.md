# Customer Engagement Strategy - Spot Pet Insurance

## Strategic Framework

### Mission-Aligned Engagement
Spot's customer engagement strategy centers on helping pets and their parents lead longer, healthier, and happier lives through strategic pre and post-purchase communication systems that drive the $350M GWP target while maintaining premium customer experience standards.

### Technology Migration Strategy
**Current State:** Transitioning from Intercom to Customer.io for improved email deliverability, cost efficiency, and technical capabilities.

**Migration Benefits:**
- Enhanced email deliverability with static IP options
- Direct SMS pricing through Twilio (no markup)
- True email marketing platform capabilities
- Reduced technical limitations
- Cost optimization compared to Intercom

## Pre-Purchase Engagement

### Lead Generation and Nurturing

#### **Customer Personas and Messaging Strategy**
Spot employs psychographic-based engagement tailored to three primary personas:

**1. Experimental Fun Seeker**
- **Messaging:** "Life's an adventure. Pet insurance shouldn't be."
- **Characteristics:** High AOV potential, wellness adoption, premium service experiences
- **Design Approach:** Bold, vibrant colors; social setting imagery
- **Content Strategy:** Upbeat tone emphasizing unique product experiences
- **Value Proposition:** Adventure-focused, confidence-building, premium brand positioning

**2. Open Simplicity Seeker**
- **Messaging:** "Pet insurance. Simple as a belly rub."
- **Characteristics:** Volume driver, straightforward solutions, clear value focus
- **Design Approach:** Light colors (blues/greens), ample white space, minimal constraints
- **Content Strategy:** Direct, simple language; minimal details; flexible approach
- **Value Proposition:** Ease and simplicity over complexity

**3. Organized Social Contributor**
- **Messaging:** "Better pets. Better humans. Better pet insurance."
- **Characteristics:** Premium segment, research-driven, word-of-mouth generation
- **Design Approach:** Warm color palette, accessible formats, authentic imagery
- **Content Strategy:** Straightforward language emphasizing caring and social responsibility
- **Value Proposition:** Social good, collaboration, making a difference

#### **Quote Form Optimization**
- **Primary Focus:** UTQ (User-to-Quote) rate improvement from 13% baseline
- **Channel Variation:** Organic (49.5% QTP) vs Aggregators (18.4% QTP) vs Social (15.3% QTP)
- **A/B Testing Framework:** Continuous testing of CTAs, images, copy, and user journey logic
- **Recent Success:** Spot Perks display optimization achieved 14% QTP increase

#### **Multi-Channel Acquisition Strategy**
- **Organic:** $1 CPA with highest conversion rates (49.5% QTP)
- **Aggregators:** 56.8% volume share, $280 CPA, optimization needed for 18.4% QTP
- **Social Media:** $675 CPA, 15.3% QTP requiring strategic improvement
- **Employer Benefits:** $4 CPA during Nov-Dec open enrollment optimization
- **Sam's Club Partnership:** 15% discount offering with early-stage development

## Post-Purchase Engagement

### Customer Lifecycle Management

#### **Policy Active Journey (Golden Journey)**
**Immediate Activation (Day 0-1):**
- Welcome and zigzag email campaigns
- Previous care education for policy understanding
- Coverage activation confirmation with case studies

**Early Engagement (Day 15):**
- Coverage active education series
- SMS service value propositions
- Plan-specific educational content

#### **Customer Retention Strategy**
**Core Retention Metrics:**
- 30-Day Retention: 93.6% (6.4% early cancellation prevention opportunity)
- Annual Churn Target: <25% (current baseline ~30% for app users)
- Claims Experience: Primary predictor of retention and churn correlation

**Retention Enhancement Channels:**
1. **Mobile App Experience**
   - Current App Store Rating: 2.8/5 (iOS), 2.9/5 (Android)
   - Target: >4/5 rating improvement
   - Goal: 50% adoption rate (current 23%)
   - Key Features: Claims submission, billing management, pet information, push notifications

2. **Member Portal Optimization**
   - Account management and self-service capabilities
   - Payment method updates and billing frequency changes
   - Policy document access and plan modifications
   - Reimbursement preference management

#### **Lapsed Customer Recovery**
**30-Day Lapsed Journey:**
- Day 0: Payment failed urgent notification
- Day 2: Account update urgent notification
- Day 6: Payment failed direct notification
- Day 14: Account past due direct notification
- Day 24: Final attempt urgent notification

**Post-30-Day Recovery:**
- Day 1: Update payment direct notification
- Standard lapsed journey sequence with graduated urgency
- Multi-channel approach (email + SMS)
