## SPEAKER NOTES & TALKING POINTS

### Opening (Slides 1-3):
- Establish emotional connection with pets
- Everyone can relate to unexpected vet bills
- Set up the problem before presenting the solution
- Use personal anecdote if possible

### Value Proposition (Slides 4-6):
- Lead with the discount stacking (this is unique!)
- Emphasize "exclusive" and "only through your employer"
- Social proof early - Forbes, BBB, reviews
- This builds trust before talking price

### Education (Slides 7-9):
- Address the TWO biggest objections from research:
  1. "What's actually covered?" (28% confused)
  2. "How does reimbursement work?" (22% unclear)
- Keep it simple - use visuals
- Repeat "go to any vet" multiple times (key differentiator)

### Pricing & Value (Slides 10-11):
- Break down to daily cost (less than coffee)
- Show real examples with THEIR discount
- Multi-pet example is crucial (extra savings)
- Spot Perks is unique differentiator (14% QTP boost proven)

### Conversion (Slides 12-18):
- Stack every reason to enroll NOW
- Create urgency (enrollment window, pet aging, pre-existing conditions)
- Social proof from similar employees
- Remove friction with simple enrollment process

### Closing (Slides 19-21):
- Make enrollment STUPID simple
- Provide multiple contact methods
- Reinforce deadline and unique value
- End with emotional connection to pets
