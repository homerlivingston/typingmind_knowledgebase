**Slide 3: How It Works**

# Protection Score System (0-100 points)

**4 Parameters Drive Armor Level:**
- Annual Limit (30 pts): $5k ‚Üí $10k ‚Üí Unlimited
- Reimbursement % (30 pts): 70% ‚Üí 80% ‚Üí 90%
- Deductible (25 pts): $1,000 ‚Üí $500 ‚Üí $250 (inverse scoring)
- Wellness Tier (15 pts): None ‚Üí Gold ‚Üí Platinum

**Visual Result:**
- 0-25 pts = Light armor (vest)
- 26-50 pts = Medium armor (vest + collar)
- 51-75 pts = Heavy armor (full protection)
- 76-100 pts = Ultimate knight mode + celebration effects!
