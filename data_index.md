# Data Repository Index - Spot Pet Insurance

## Overview
This directory contains all performance data, analytics, and forecasts for Spot Pet Insurance operations.

## Data Files

### Channel Performance Data
- `policies_trend.csv` - Monthly policy distribution by channel (Dec 2024 - Aug 2025)
- `cpa_trends.csv` - Cost Per Acquisition trends by channel with quote volume data
- `channel_level_comparison.csv` - Comprehensive channel comparison with LTV, conversion rates, cancellation rates

### Marketing Mix Modeling & Spend Forecasts
- `august_spend_forecast.csv` - MMM attribution and spend plans for August 2025
- `september_spend_forecast.csv` - MMM attribution and spend plans for September 2025

### Customer Survey Data
- `post_purchase_survey.csv` - Post-purchase customer satisfaction and decision factors (2,461 responses)
- `quoter_survey.csv` - Non-converting quoter feedback and barriers (500+ responses)
- `cancel_survey.csv` - Customer cancellation reasons and experience feedback (200+ responses)
- `unknown_cancels_survey.csv` - Additional cancellation data and insights
- `customer_survey.csv` - Recent customer experience and satisfaction data

## Key Data Points

### Current Performance (Aug 2025)
- **Total Monthly Policies:** 25,705 conversions
- **Blended CPA:** $204 (down from $250 in Dec 2024)
- **Top Volume Channel:** Aggregator (56.8% share, $280 CPA)
- **Best Economics:** Employer/Broker ($4 CPA), Organic ($1 CPA)
- **Problem Channel:** Paid Social ($675 CPA, poor conversion rates)

### MMM Insights
- **Highest ROAS:** Google Brand (8.1), Mountain (8.4)
- **Growth Opportunity:** Facebook maintaining strong 6.0 ROAS
- **Efficiency Concern:** TikTok at 2.7 ROAS, Instagram at 3.0 ROAS
- **Testing Opportunities:** 247 Ads (3.9 ROAS), Nextdoor (1.1 ROAS)

## Data Usage Notes
- All channel data includes First Touch attribution
- CPA calculations include both direct costs and attributed conversions
- LTV projections based on 4-year average customer lifetime
- Cancel rates measured at 30-day mark post-policy activation
- ROAS calculations include expected, next $20k spend, and marginal dollar ROAS

### Key Survey Insights

#### Customer Decision Factors (Post-Purchase)
- **Better Price:** 31.8% chose Spot for competitive pricing
- **Comprehensive Coverage:** 32.0% chose Spot for coverage quality
- **Recommendations:** 23.7% influenced by friends/family
- **Loyalty:** 54% plan to keep insurance "for life"

#### Conversion Barriers (Quoter Survey)
- **Price Sensitivity:** 35% cite "too expensive"
- **Coverage Confusion:** 28% unclear on what's covered
- **Reimbursement Process:** 22% don't understand how it works
- **Desired Features:** Direct vet payment most requested

#### Churn Drivers (Cancellation Surveys)
- **Claim Denials:** 45% of cancellations (pre-existing condition disputes)
- **Rate Increases:** 25% cite premium increases
- **Low Reimbursements:** 28% disappointed with payout amounts
- **Process Frustration:** Documentation requests, slow processing

## Strategic Implications
This comprehensive data supports the $350M GWP goal by identifying:
1. **Channel Optimization:** Fix Paid Social ($675 CPA), scale Facebook vs TikTok
2. **Scale Opportunities:** Aggregator efficiency, Benefit Platform growth
3. **Retention Focus:** Claims experience improvement (#1 priority)
4. **Conversion Optimization:** Address coverage confusion, simplify reimbursement explanation
