# Spot Pet Insurance — Product Overview

## Core Plans
- **Accident & Illness (A&I)** — Covers accidents, illnesses, diagnostics (X-rays/MRI/CT), surgery, hospitalization, prescription meds/special diets, behavioral, hereditary & chronic, microchip.
- **Accident-Only** — Accident events only; excludes illnesses.

## Add-ons
- **Preventive Care (Wellness) — Gold / Platinum**: Routine care; reimbursements are schedule-based.

## Key Benefits (high level)
- **Any licensed vet** in the U.S./Canada (including specialists/emergency).
- **24/7 VetAccess** tele-vet.
- **Customizable coverage**: Annual Limit (e.g., $2.5k → Unlimited), Reimbursement (70/80/90%), Deductible ($100 → $1,000).
- **Multi-pet discount** (10%).

## Typical Waiting Periods (examples)
- **A&I:** 14 days.
- **Preventive:** starts the day after enrollment.
(Confirm state variations; see `legal/disclosures.md`.)

## Step Flow
- **Step 1**: Intake (pet + contact).
- **Step 2**: Coverage selection & pricing.
- **Purchase** → **Thank You** (account creation + perks) → Member Center.
